All data used to generate this page is anonymized and stripped of any personally identifiable information. The data represents a subset of CTL's conversations.

The visualizations above are representative of teens who text Crisis Text Line, not necessarily of the national teen population. For example, this may mean that "suicidal ideation" is more common in states like Montana, but it could also mean that teens in Montana are more likely to reach out for help generally or reach out to us specifically.
